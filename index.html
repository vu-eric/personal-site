About

I am a software engineer currently based in San Jose, California, with experience in JavaScript/Node.js, Ruby, PostgreSQL, AWS, Docker, and React.
Most recently, I co-created Signet, a contract testing framework for microservices.

Signet

Signet is an open-source framework that makes it simple to set up contract testing for microservices. Signet automates the contract testing workflow, is deployment-aware, and integrates with CI/CD pipelines.

It is designed for teams that are experiencing the pain points of integration and E2E testing, and want an easy way to introduce contract testing. Read the Case Study
